Sept 2009-March 2018

I fulfilled two positions while working for InfoPro Corporation. The first was as an Instructor at the Navy's Explosive Ordnance Disposal (EOD) school. This was an extremely rewarding position because I knew the knowledge I was imparting on the troops could not only save their lives but also allow them to save others. The second position was as an Unexploded Ordnance (UXO) technician on Eglin Air Force Base. Here I assisted and supported testing of most new explosive weaponry that our troops would use world wide. The weaponry testing was extremely diverse ranging anywhere from testing propellant composition of small arms ammunition, conducting new explosive characteristics testing, to connecting a few dozen rockets to a 2000 pound bomb and sending it through simulated buildings and bunkers to optimize warhead performance.

Summary of Accomplishments:

  • Delivered over 200 classes in identification and procedures for grenades, rockets, projectiles, and landmines
  • Taught Explosive Ordnance Disposal troops from over 12 different nations
  • Developed and maintained curriculum for classroom, practical, and testing areas
  • Supported testing of next generation weapons for Air Force Research Laboratories
  • Advised researchers on testing methodologies and data acquisition for explosive article testing
  • Maintained a Class B CDL with HAZMAT endorsement and specialized in shipping requirements and transporting explosive articles
